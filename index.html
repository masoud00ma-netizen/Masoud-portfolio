About

Behind the Timeline

From raw clips to a finished story — here's how I work.

I'm a video editor focused on short videos — built for quick, punchy storytelling that hooks viewers in the first second and holds their attention until the last.

I also edit real estate videos: cinematic property walkthroughs, drone footage cuts, and listing reels that highlight a home's best features and help it sell faster.

I use After Effects as my main editing tool — building the entire cut, motion graphics, animated captions and transitions inside AE for full creative control.

Premiere Pro handles rough cuts, multi-track sound design and color grading, while Photoshop covers thumbnails, cover frames and any graphic elements the project needs.
